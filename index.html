About Tiny

Did you know that Tiny was intended to be just a bot on a friends server? No? Now you do! Tiny started to be developed in 2018, and, although it was started as a test project, it ended up providing great support to the osu! community for 4 long years, with many updates and more!

A great variety of commands!

The bot has unique features, such as:

  • Track your best plays on the channel of your choice.
  • Compare your friends plays.
  • Have fun with the osu cards.
  • Send a map file and get information about the map.
  • Get information from a map based on a screenshot!

Not only that, there are a lot of other features, and, although most of them are linked to the game, there are others that are not, add the bot to your server to discover them all!

Commands

Still not convinced? Check out some examples of the most used bot commands!

  • osuset

    Link your osu! account with your Discord account, so you don't have to worry about entering your nickname every time you use the commands

  • recent

    Want to show your friends your most recent play? Use this command and get additional information!

  • card

    Fan of card games? Now you can have your own card! Using this command you can generate a card containing all your skills!

  • osu

    Do you want to view your profile statistics without logging into the game? Of course you can! Using this command, you will be able to see all the information you are looking for and even generate a graph of your performance in the game!

Privacy Policy

  • Data Storage and Usage

    The bot stores publicly available information such as: game username, Discord user id. It also stores information about the server where the bot is located (ONLY IF SOME CHANGES ARE MADE TO THE BOT OR CERTAIN FUNCTIONS ARE USED, FOR EXAMPLE CHANGING PREFIXES OR USING THE TRACKING FUNCTION).

    This information is used for the sole purpose of obtaining the required game data, sending messages to the selected channels or keeping track of the selected prefix. The information is stored in a database managed by us, which is protected by 2-Auth key access and only we have access to it.

    Your information is stored indefinitely in our database as it is necessary for commands that you can use without any mandatory use of parameters, such as getting your own game profile, tracking your moves in order to show the best ones, etc.

    If you want to remove your information such as your Discord username or nickname from the database, you can do so by joining the Discord server.

  • Message Content Usage

    We do not store information about the messages sent to the different servers where the bot is located. As we said before, the only information stored is related to the game, the Discord user ID or the server used only for the purpose of providing the different features that the bot contains.

    We make use of the content of the messages to perform certain actions such as: Existence or not of content related to the other bot, existence or not of links related to the game, existence or not of parameters related to the latter or to some attachment related to the game and existence or not of bot command.

    If you own a server and do not want this information to be tracked, you can reduce the permissions of the bot in different channels, leaving it in one related to the game so that the bot performs the checks only in that channel.
    If you are a server user, you have the right not to send any message in the bot related channels.

  • Discord Data Usage

    The bot contains some commands that make use of the Discord public API to obtain public information such as: your avatar, your discord tag or your id. This information is not shared between servers and these commands can only be used within a server and the person must be on the same server, your data is not shared with any commercial or related entity.

    We are not responsible for any screenshot or leakage of this data that may happen because of you or a third party member.
